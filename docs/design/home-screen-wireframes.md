# Home Screen Wireframes

## Main Areas

- Top: Avatar, companion, level, streak, life balance snapshot
- Center: Today's 3 main quests, 2 side quests, "best next step" button, animated world view
- Bottom: Focus timer, "campfire reflection" shortcut, "my strategy" profile shortcut, event feed

## Wireframe Descriptions

### Top Area

- Avatar and companion with level and streak indicator
- Life balance snapshot (pie or bar chart)

### Center Area

- List of today's main quests and side quests
- Animated world view reflecting recent progress
- "Best next step" prominent CTA

### Bottom Area

- Focus timer/start quest button
- Shortcuts to reflection and strategy profile
- Subtle event feed (e.g., "Your garden grew after your morning walk.")
