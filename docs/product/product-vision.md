# Time-Forge: Life Quest — Product Vision

Time-Forge: Life Quest is a mobile-first gamified life and time-management platform that uses game loops, world-building, adaptive quests, and private coaching insights to help users improve planning, execution, focus, and life balance.

## Major Surfaces

- Player Surface (Flutter mobile game-like experience)
- Professional / Pro Surface (structured productivity view)
- Personal Guidance Surface (private strategy profile)
- Administrative / Analytics Surface (Angular-based command center)

## Product Principles

- Reward useful behavior, not just task completion
- Make planning and reflection emotionally acceptable
- Tailor the world to the user’s real life
- Keep personal strategy and life-profile data private by default
- Build the engine seriously and the surface playfully
- Use gamification to make life-management approachable
